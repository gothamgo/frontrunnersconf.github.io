Emcee: Steve Chen

Welcome to our host Steve Chen!

Our emcee for the day will be Steve Chen. Steve is heavily involved in the DC and New York meetup scenes. He organizes the DC React meetup group. He is also the Community Executive Officer at Code & Coffee, which is a series of community-led events appearing in 21 US cities and growing!

Thank you, Steve, for helping us bring back in person events to DC and starting by hosting Frontrunners!
