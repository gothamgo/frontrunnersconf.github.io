Welcome to the Frontrunners Conference!

Frontrunners is an annual JavaScript and web developers conference held in the Washington, DC area! This year Frontrunners will be Dec 8, 2022 in Arlington, VA at GMU's Van Metre Hall (formerly Founders Hall).

We want to hear your talk!

Our call for talks is open! Submit your talk! We expect 250 developers to attend Frontrunners. So, this is a great opportunity to reach a large and influential audience of DC web developers, and have a great time, too!
